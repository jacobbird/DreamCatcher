  Tom Wentz C.A.M.F.  
 

          Tom is an educator and published author who has lead research in the field of alcohol and drug related disorders. He has been employed in the field of Drug and Alcohol rehabilitation for the past 20 years. He holds a Ph.D. in special education and his areas of expertise include addiction counseling and alcohol related disease. He recieved post- doctoral training at the Hazelden Addiction Treatment and Research Facility where he studied family interactions and treatment programs. Tom later bacame a Lead Couselor III at the Betty Ford Center's Profressionals in Recovery Program. Tom, on a personal note, has been drug and alcohol free for 20 years. He credits his sobriety to strong community and continued participation in process of recovery.
